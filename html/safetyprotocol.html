Operational Guidelines

Warehouse Operations

Zone A
Pedestrian Safety

Walk only on designated Green Pathways. Establish eye contact with forklift operators before crossing intersections.

Stacking Height

Maximum stack height for MS Bags is 3 Layers. Ensure pyramids are stable before removing shrink wrap.

LOTO Procedure

Lock Out Tag Out mandatory during conveyor maintenance. Only authorized electrical personnel may remove tags.

H2S Monitoring

Personal gas monitors required near sulfur pits. Evacuate immediately if alarm triggers (Sound + Vibration).

Pier & Loading

Zone B

Life Vests: Mandatory within 2 meters of the pier edge. No exceptions.

Suspended Loads: Never walk under crane loads. Maintain a 10-meter exclusion zone during active lifting.

Weather Protocol: Operations cease immediately if wind speed exceeds 25 knots or lightning is detected within 10km.

IN CASE OF EMERGENCY

Stay calm. Follow the evacuation route. Do not use elevators.
