Quality Excellence Pipeline

Quality by MS Product

Explore how our Quality Group enforces strict analytical checks at every stage of the manufacturing lifecycle.

Stage 01 • In-Process Control

In-Process Quality Control (IPQC)

Continuous monitoring of manufacturing parameters and assembly precision in real-time. By catching variance early, we guarantee zero-defect tolerance across all MS Product assembly lines.

  • Hourly dimensional verification & sampling
  • Automated statistical process control (SPC) alerts
Stage 02 • Final Auditing

Finished Goods Reliability Testing

Before deployment, completed MS Products undergo rigorous stress testing, functional diagnostics, and visual compliance inspection to ensure they exceed international quality standards.

  • 100% final functional & safety validation
  • Digital Certificate of Analysis (COA) generation
Stage 03 • Vendor Audit

Raw Material & Vendor Verification

Quality begins at the source. We enforce strict supplier quality audits and conduct chemical/physical lab testing on all incoming shipments before they enter the MS Section pipeline.

  • Raw material lot traceability & quarantine control
  • Vendor scorecard & rating integration
