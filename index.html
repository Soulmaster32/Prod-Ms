Automate Employee Onboarding Equipment Dispatch

SJ
Submitted by Sarah Jenkins on Oct 10, 2026

The Problem Statement

New hires currently wait an average of 4 days to receive their laptops and security keys due to manual IT triage emails between HR and IT. Whenever an employee is added to Workday, an HR coordinator has to manually copy-paste their equipment requirements into an email to the IT helpdesk, resulting in missed tickets, human errors, and significant onboarding friction.

Proposed Solution

Integrate our Workday HR system directly with the ServiceNow hardware dispatch API using an automated webhook. When a new hire is marked as "Hired" in Workday, a ticket with the correct hardware profile (e.g., Mac vs PC, required peripherals) should automatically generate in ServiceNow and route directly to the dispatch queue.
